Tips for Submitting Your Recipe

Title

Choose a clear title that tells the cook what to expect and includes search-friendly words. "Andouille and Chicken Creole Pasta" tells us more about the dish than "Mardi Gras Pasta."

Description

Include a short but detailed sentence describing the dish that will get the cook excited and tell them what to expect.

"This pan-seared tilapia dish is a delicious and easy way to prepare seafood! Great for a quick weeknight meal accompanied with fresh veggies."
